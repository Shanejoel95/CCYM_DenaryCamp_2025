CCYM Upcountry Deanery

Notice

✔ Bring your own Bible, Notebook & Pen – Essential for study sessions, discussions, and note-taking.
✔ Bring your own Personal Toiletries & Medications – Bring your own soap, toothpaste, towel, and any prescribed medications.
✔ Bring Comfortable Clothing – Pack clothes suitable for all weather conditions and activities.
✔ Bring Sportswear & Sneakers – Required for Sports Day and other physical activities.
✔ Bring Reusable Water Bottle – Stay hydrated throughout the camp.
✔ Sleeping Bag or Bedding – If necessary, based on the camp accommodations.
✔ Flashlight & Extra Batteries – Useful for nighttime activities and emergencies.
❌ Do not bring Expensive Items – Avoid bringing jewelry, laptops, or large amounts of cash to prevent loss or damage.
❌ Electronic Devices – Phones and other devices should only be used for necessary communication.
❌ Do not bring Disruptive Items – Any harmful, inappropriate, or distracting objects that may affect the camp environment.
Be prepared for physical activities by bringing suitable sportswear and shoes.
Participation in team-building exercises and games is encouraged.
Follow the instructions of camp leaders for a safe and enjoyable experience.
Show respect to camp leaders, fellow campers, and camp property.
Follow the camp schedule and actively participate in sessions, prayers, and discussions.
Maintain a positive and Christ-centered attitude in all activities.
Keep the camp environment clean by disposing of waste properly.
Arrive on time and check in at the registration desk.
If you have any food allergies or medical concerns, inform the camp organizers in advance.
